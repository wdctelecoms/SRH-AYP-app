PEER-TO-PEER SUPPORT FORUMS

Empowering youths with safe platforms to connect, share experiences, and access Sexual & Reproductive Health (SRH) services. Your Health. Your Right. Your Future.

Our Key Strategies

Establish Safe & Inclusive Spaces

  • We connect you to physical and virtual environments where individuals feel safe sharing experiences.
  • We ensure confidentiality and privacy when dealing with sensitive topics.
  • We foster a strong sense of belonging and community.
Find Services

Provide Proper Peer Supporters

  • We refer you to trained peer supporters with accurate information and strong communication skills.
  • Our supporters identify when specialized care or referrals are needed.

Utilize Diverse Platforms

  • We combine online and offline approaches to reach diverse audiences.
  • We use social media, online forums, and mobile messaging.
  • We organize in-person sessions, workshops, and community events.

Engage Community Leaders & Influencers

  • We partner with community leaders, religious figures, and influencers.
  • We involve parents and guardians to build supportive environments.
  • We use community events and media to reduce stigma.

Promote Positive Social Norms

  • We challenge harmful beliefs through peer education.
  • We promote open communication within families and communities.
  • We encourage healthy SRH behaviors among young people.

Feedback & Evaluation

  • We regularly collect feedback from participants.
  • We evaluate the impact of peer support programs.
  • We use insights to continuously improve our initiatives.
