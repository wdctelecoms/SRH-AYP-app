PEER-TO-PEER SUPPORT FORUMS

Equiping youths with proper platforms to allow them network in accessing health services, for Your Health, Your Right, Your Future

Strategies we are taking to enhance peer-to-peer support in SRH:

Establish safe and inclusive spaces:

  • We connect you to physical and virtual environments where individuals feel comfortable sharing their experiences and seeking support without judgment.
  • Ensure confidentiality and privacy, especially when dealing with sensitive topics.
  • Foster a sense of belonging and community among participants.

Provide Proper proper peer supporters

  • Ensure we get to refer you to peer supporters with accurate,information,communication skills and have resources that help effectively guide them.
  • Provide trained peer supporters that identifies and responds to individuals in need of specialized support or referral services.

Utilize diverse platforms

  • We Combine online and offline approaches to reach a wider audience and cater to different preferences.
    • <
  • We use social media, online forums, and mobile messaging for peer-to-peer interaction and information sharing.
  • Help you connect to Organize in-person sessions,find support groups, workshops, and community events to facilitate face-to-face connections.

Engage community leaders and influencers

  • We are Partnering with community leaders, religious figures, and influencers to promote peer support and reduce stigma associated with SRH
  • We are trying to Involve parents, guardians, and other influential figures in the community to create a supportive environment for young people accessing SRH services,
  • We are also Utilize community events and media to raise awareness about the importance of peer support and reduce stigma.

Promote positive social norms

  • We are Challenging harmful social norms and beliefs related to SRH through peer education and community engagement.
  • We are Promoting open communication and dialogue about SRH within families and communities.
  • We are Encourage healthy behaviors and attitudes towards SRH among young people.

Incorporate feedback and evaluation

  • We Regularly collect feedback from peer supporters and recipients to identify areas for improvement.
  • We Evaluate the impact of peer support programs on knowledge, attitudes, and behaviors related to SRH.
  • We Use evaluation findings to refine and enhance the effectiveness of peer support initiatives.
