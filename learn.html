What is Sexual and Reproductive Health (SRH)?

SRH covers the well-being of your body, relationships, safety, and choices. It includes access to correct info, safe practices, and knowing your rights.

Puberty & Changes

Puberty is a natural process where your body changes — both physically and emotionally. It’s different for everyone. Learn to understand and care for yourself.

Consent & Respect

Consent means freely agreeing to something — especially in relationships. Always ask, and always respect the other person’s answer.

STIs & Protection

Use protection like condoms to prevent sexually transmitted infections. Know the facts — don’t let myths mislead you.

Where to Get Help

If you need support, talk to a trusted adult, health worker, or visit a youth-friendly clinic. Your health matters!
